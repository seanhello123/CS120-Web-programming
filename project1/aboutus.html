Meet our band members!

Adam Levine

Vocals. A renowned singer, songwriter, and musician. With his distinctively smooth and soulful voice. Adam Levine has captivated audiences around the world. His vocal range, combined with his charismatic stage presence, has made him one of the most recognized and admired musicians of his generation.

James Valentine

The lead guitarist for the band. He brings his exceptional musical skills and unique style to our signature sound. His dynamic guitar riffs and captivating solos have become synonymous with the band's infectious melodies, adding depth and texture to our music.

Matt Flynn

Exceptionally talented and skilled drummer behind the rhythmic heartbeat of the internationally acclaimed pop rock band Metro Music Band. Matt Flynn has been a driving force in delivering the band's infectious beats and dynamic energy.
